Yuji Itadori
A high school student with extraordinary physical abilities who becomes involved in the world of jujutsu sorcery after swallowing a powerful cursed object, Ryomen Sukuna's finger. Despite the dangers, he continues to fight to protect others and uphold his grandfather's dying wish to help people.
Megumi Fushigiro
Megumi is a first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High and a member of the Zenin Clan. He is a skilled jujutsu sorcerer who uses the Ten Shadows Technique, which allows him to summon shikigami (spiritual familiars) to fight. He is calm, serious, and values the lives of others.
Nobara Kugisaki
Nobara is another first-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High. She is a bold and confident sorcerer who uses a combination of a hammer and nails infused with cursed energy in combat. Nobara is fiercely independent and has a strong sense of self, aiming to stay true to her identity while fighting curses.
Toge Inumaki
Toge is a second-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High. He possesses the Cursed Speech technique, which allows him to imbue his words with cursed energy to force others to obey his commands. Due to the dangerous nature of his ability, he often speaks using safe ingredients of rice balls to avoid accidental harm.
Maki Zenin
LMaki is a second-year student at Tokyo Jujutsu High and a member of the Zenin Clan. Despite being born without cursed energy, she is a formidable sorcerer due to her exceptional physical prowess and mastery of cursed tools. Maki is determined to prove her worth and overcome the limitations imposed by her family.
Panda
Panda is a unique, sentient cursed corpse created by Principal Masamichi Yaga. Despite his appearance, Panda is intelligent and capable of human speech. He has three cores (panda, gorilla, and a yet-to-be-revealed form) that give him different abilities and fighting styles.
Yuta Okkotsu
Yuta is a special grade jujutsu sorcerer and the protagonist of the prequel series "Jujutsu Kaisen 0". He was initially cursed by his childhood friend Rika Orimoto, who manifests as a powerful and vengeful spirit. Under the guidance of Satoru Gojo, Yuta learns to control Rika's power and becomes a skilled sorcerer in his own right.
